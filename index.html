Water Scientist



About me


I work at water resources engineering and hydro-informatics interface.

My research agenda includes advancing the understanding and physical representation of nutrients' fate and transport to manage water resources using the computing infrastructure. I am also interested in exploring how interactions between climate and land management operations impact water quantity and quality.

My passion for working on water resources, precisely water quality, is driven by the idea that resolving water problems can solve much unseen unpredicted chaos in the ecosystem. I love to work as a problem solver on issues related to water sustainability.
